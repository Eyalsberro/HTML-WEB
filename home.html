About Me

I was caught up in a web of negativity and needed someone or something to help me to escape.

A bit about myself: I'm Eyal Sberro from Israel, I got my first camera at the age of 10. and I got this passion for photography since then. I love to take pictures of nature, landscape, Sunsets, and urban pictures from an unusual angle.I am surrounded both by natural beauty and interesting people. I use my camera to document the world and then print images with a focus on the magic I find in daily life. My pictures tell my stories..
